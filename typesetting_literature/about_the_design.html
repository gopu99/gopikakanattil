Why The history of the pencil?

Pencils always fascinated me, different strokes, marks, shades that can be made, even the shape and colour of different pencils always excited me. As an artist I always prefer pencils over any other drawing media. When I was asked to select a topic for my typesetting project I was dead set on history of pencils.

Font and Colour

Fonts I used for this website are Georgia and IM Fell English SC. Since its about history I thought serif fonts will give the article an old world charm. I selected georgia for paragraph because it is known for the clarity on computer monitor even at small sizes. For selecting colours I created a mood board with all the pictures from the original article and a list of colours used in the pictures was created. After some trials I finalised a radial gradient for the main heading in the home page with colours grayish green and light grayish yellow. And the other colours are grayish green, light grayish yellow, very dark garyish red, white and black.
